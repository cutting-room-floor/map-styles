DC NIGHTVISION

== PREREQUISITES ==========================================

While you may modify these styles to work with any data, to use them as
they are you will need to download the following files.

---- Shapefiles -------------------------------------------

DC boundary polygons
http://dcatlas.dcgis.dc.gov/catalog/download.asp?downloadID=125&downloadTYPE=ESRI

DC buildings polygons
http://dcatlas.dcgis.dc.gov/catalog/download.asp?downloadID=1021&downloadTYPE=ESRI

DC metro station points
http://dcatlas.dcgis.dc.gov/catalog/download.asp?downloadID=27&downloadTYPE=ESRI

DC OpenStreetMap roads
http://downloads.cloudmade.com/north_america/united_states/district_of_columbia/district_of_columbia.shapefiles.zip

DC parks polygons
http://dcatlas.dcgis.dc.gov/catalog/download.asp?downloadID=133&downloadTYPE=ESRI

DC roads polygons
http://dcatlas.dcgis.dc.gov/catalog/download.asp?downloadID=82&downloadTYPE=ESRI

DC topography, one meter contour lines
http://dcatlas.dcgis.dc.gov/catalog/download.asp?downloadID=94&downloadTYPE=ESRI

DC water polygons
http://dcatlas.dcgis.dc.gov/catalog/download.asp?downloadID=104&downloadTYPE=ESRI

---- Fonts ------------------------------------------------

Liberation Sans Regular
https://fedorahosted.org/releases/l/i/liberation-fonts/liberation-fonts-ttf-1.05.1.20090721.zip
